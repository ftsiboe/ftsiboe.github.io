## Public 🔓

- [USFarmSafetyNetLab](https://github.com/ftsiboe/USFarmSafetyNetLab):
  Analytical tools and resources dedicated to United States agricultural
  safety net programs.
- [rfcipDemand](https://github.com/ftsiboe/rfcipDemand): Tools to
  estimate Federal Crop Insurance Program (FCIP) demand.
- [rexcelbridge](https://github.com/ftsiboe/rexcelbridge):
  Vendor-neutral helpers to evaluate Excel formulas from R via COM
  automation.

## Private 🔒

- [rfcipCalcPass](https://github.com/ftsiboe/ftsiboe/tree/main/data-raw/private-packages/rfcipCalcPass.md):
  R implementations of FCIP premium and indemnity calculators.
- [rfcipCalibrate](https://github.com/ftsiboe/ftsiboe/tree/main/data-raw/private-packages/rfcipCalibrate.md):
  R tools for calibrating yield and preference models for farm-level
  FCIP experience.
- [rfcipCalibrateExtended](https://github.com/ftsiboe/ftsiboe/tree/main/data-raw/private-packages/rfcipCalibrateExtended.md):
  Extended tools for rfcipCalibrate.
- [rfcipReSim](https://github.com/ftsiboe/ftsiboe/tree/main/data-raw/private-packages/rfcipReSim.md):
  R modular simulator for FCIP reinsurance outcomes.
- [rfcipPRF](https://github.com/ftsiboe/ftsiboe/tree/main/data-raw/private-packages/rfcipPRF.md):
  Tools for RMA Pasture, Rangeland, and Forage (PRF) insurance data and
  policy analysis.
- [rkfmaData](https://github.com/ftsiboe/ftsiboe/tree/main/data-raw/private-packages/rkfmaData.md):
  Tools for the Kansas Farm Management Data Bank.
- [rAgroClimate](https://github.com/ftsiboe/ftsiboe/tree/main/data-raw/private-packages/rAgroClimate.md):
  Agroclimatic and environmental datasets for agricultural research.
- [arpcPriceBasis](https://github.com/ftsiboe/ftsiboe/tree/main/data-raw/private-packages/arpcPriceBasis.md):
  R tools for price and basis by commodity and county/elevator.
- [arpcCost](https://github.com/ftsiboe/ftsiboe/tree/main/data-raw/private-packages/arpcCost.md):
  R tools to calibrate and harmonize agricultural production costs by
  commodity and county.
- [arpcFCIP](https://github.com/ftsiboe/ftsiboe/tree/main/data-raw/private-packages/arpcFCIP.md):
  Simulate and benchmark Federal Crop Insurance Program (FCIP) outcomes.
